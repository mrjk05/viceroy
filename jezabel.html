JEZABEL 

Designed for the woman who seeks to journey to the world's most idyllic beaches in search of a tranquil respite from the pressures of everyday life, Jezebel is the perfect accessory for those seeking both style and sustainability. Jezebel is both understated and elegant, making it the perfect complement to any outfit.

The high-quality lenses of Jezebel provide crystal-clear vision while protecting the wearer against the harsh glare of the sun's rays. Each pair is meticulously crafted to ensure durability and longevity, capable of withstanding the rigors of even the most challenging of travels.

Jezebel is a testament to the beauty of eco-conscious design. Its materials are thoughtfully sourced and sustainably harvested, embodying a commitment to both beauty and the preservation of the natural world. The hues of the frames are inspired by the radiant colors of the Jezebel butterfly, evoking a sense of wonder and delight in all those who behold them.
