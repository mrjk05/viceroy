V I C E  R O Y 

ViceRoy is a brand born from a desire to celebrate the beauty of transformation and the freedom to be new in every moment. Drawing inspiration from the butterfly, a symbol of renewal and rebirth, ViceRoy believes that the journey of personal growth is one of the most beautiful and meaningful experiences that life has to offer. ViceRoy encourages its customers to connect with their roots while embarking on new adventures, to find a balance between honoring tradition and embracing innovation. At ViceRoy, sustainability is at the forefront of everything we do. We believe that fashion should not come at the expense of our planet, and we strive to make a positive impact on the environment with every pair of wooden sunglasses we create. Our commitment to ethical production and the use of sustainable materials allows our customers to enjoy fashion with a clean conscience, knowing that their purchase is making a difference. Our bespoke wooden sunglasses are a one-of-a-kind accessory that reflect the daring spirit of our customers. Be it exploring through mountiainous terrains or lounging on sandy beaches, ViceRoy sunglasses make for the perfect companion to anyone seeking to experience the magnificence of the world around them.

We believe that life is a journey of self-discovery and personal growth. Our brand is a celebration of that journey, a reminder to embrace the freedom of being new in every moment while staying connected to our roots. We invite you to join us on this journey and to experience the beauty of transformation with ViceRoy sunglasses.
